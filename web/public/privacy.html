Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2024

GitConnect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our voice-powered GitHub editor service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with GitHub, we receive:

  • Your GitHub user ID and username
  • Your email address (if public on GitHub)
  • Access to repositories you authorize

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use GitConnect:

  • API usage counts (for subscription limits)
  • Features accessed
  • Error logs for debugging

Voice Data

When you use voice features:

  • Audio is streamed to Deepgram for transcription
  • We do not store audio recordings
  • Transcriptions are processed in real-time and not permanently stored

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers. We only store:

  • Stripe customer ID
  • Subscription status
  • Subscription period dates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the GitConnect service
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Track usage for subscription limits
  • Improve our service and fix bugs
  • Send important service updates (not marketing)

3. Third-Party Services

GitConnect integrates with:

  • GitHub — For authentication and repository access
  • Deepgram — For voice transcription
  • Anthropic (Claude) — For AI code generation
  • Stripe — For payment processing
  • Supabase — For user data storage
  • Netlify — For hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure token storage in your browser's localStorage

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store:

  • GitHub authentication token
  • Your API keys (if you provide them)
  • User preferences (Claude model selection)

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that identify individual users.

7. Children's Privacy

GitConnect is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@gitconnect.pro
