## Color Palette Suggestions

- **Primary**: Neon Blue / Cyan (#00D9FF or #0099FF)
- **Secondary**: Slate Gray (#475569 or #64748B)
- **Accent**: Deep Purple (#6D28D9) or Teal (#14B8A6)
- **Background**: Dark Slate (#1E293B)

---

## Style Guidelines

- **Simplicity**: 2-3 core shapes maximum
- **Scalability**: Works at 16x16 favicon size
- **Geometric**: Clean lines, no curves unless necessary
- **Tech-Forward**: Modern, not retro-looking
- **Zen**: Balanced, breathing space, not cluttered
- **90s Vibe**: Subtle neon accents, geometric precision

---

## Usage Notes

These prompts are designed for:
- Favicon (16x16, 32x32, 64x64)
- Social media profile pictures
- App icon
- Website branding

Generate multiple variations and select the one that best captures the minimalist tech aesthetic while maintaining the 90s retrofuturism feel.
